BODY LANGUAGE MASTERY

Welcome to your free Body Language Mastery course by CX Universe. Learn the art of silent communication and emotional intelligence.

🧍 Step 1: Learn to Read Posture, Gestures & Eye Movement

Start by observing how people sit, stand, and move. Are they open or closed off? Confident or nervous? 👁️‍🗨️ Watch the eyes — darting eyes may indicate lying, while steady gaze shows confidence.

🧠 Step 2: No Gadgets — Just Natural Observation

Forget devices and sensors. The human brain is the best analyzer. Observe patterns, reactions, body tension. Trust your instincts — it's pure human behavior science.

🔍 Step 3: Spot Confidence, Lies, Anxiety

Look for:

  • Fake smiles that don’t reach the eyes
  • Fidgeting or touching face = stress or lying
  • Expansive posture = dominance

💡 Step 4: Apply it in Real Life

Use this skill in interviews, business meetings, leadership roles, or even dating. Decode what people truly mean — beyond their words.
