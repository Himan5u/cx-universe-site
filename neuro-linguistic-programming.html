🧠 Step 1: Understand the NLP Basics

What You'll Learn: How language, thoughts & behavior connect — and how to reprogram them.

Examples:
  • Words like “I can’t” program failure
  • “I choose to...” builds power
  • Mindset shift = life shift
  • Focus = Energy Direction
Practice Tip: Replace every “I can’t” this week with “I’m learning to”.
Quiz: What phrase creates empowerment?
a) I hate this
b) I can’t do this
c) I’m learning to ✅
d) This always happens to me
Real-Life Mission: Catch your language — shift it mid-sentence for 1 day.
