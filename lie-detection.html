What is Lie Detection?

Lie detection is the ability to identify when someone is not telling the truth by observing their body language, facial expressions, and voice tone. It's based on psychology and human behavior.

Top 5 Signs of a Liar üîç

  • Eye Movement: Liars often avoid eye contact or blink rapidly.
  • Lip Biting: A nervous gesture when someone is uncomfortable while lying.
  • Voice Pitch: Their voice may become higher due to tension.
  • Shoulder Shrugs: Inconsistent with words, showing uncertainty.
  • Blinking Speed: Either too fast or too slow can indicate stress.
