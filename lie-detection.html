What is Lie Detection?

Lie detection is the science of identifying whether someone is telling the truth or lying — just by observing their facial expressions, body language, and voice tone.

How It Works 🔍

Our brain reacts differently when we lie. This creates micro-expressions like eye movement, lip tension, body shifts, voice pitch changes, and hand gestures. With the help of AI, we can now analyze these subtle signs quickly and more accurately.

Where It's Used 🌍

✅ Job Interviews
✅ Criminal Investigations
✅ Business Negotiations
✅ Online Communication
✅ Relationship Insights

AI Tools Used 🧠

Some AI tools and techniques used in lie detection:
• Facial Expression Recognition
• Voice Stress Analysis
• Eye Tracking Algorithms
• Sentiment Detection via NLP

Why Learn Lie Detection? 🎯

This skill boosts your observation power, increases your emotional intelligence, and makes you more confident in social situations. Whether you're a student, entrepreneur, or just curious — it's a fun and powerful life skill.

Conclusion 🚀

Master the art of truth spotting with the power of psychology and artificial intelligence. Become a modern-day lie detector — no polygraph machine needed!
